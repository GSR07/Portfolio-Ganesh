I'am Ganesh Reddy


I'm a Robotics and Machine Learning Engineer currently pursuing a Master's in Autonomous Technologies at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg. I specialize in building intelligent systems that combine mechanical design, computer vision, and embedded software.

My work involves designing and simulating robotic arms and autonomous vehicles using ROS/ROS2, SolidWorks, and Gazebo, alongside implementing control algorithms in Python and C++. I enjoy bringing together hardware and software to build real-time, sensor-driven applications—whether it's an IoT-enabled device or a computer vision system using YOLOv3 and OpenCV.

Passionate about solving real-world challenges through robotics and AI, I thrive in collaborative environments and continuously seek opportunities to apply machine learning, automation, and simulation techniques for impactful innovation.
